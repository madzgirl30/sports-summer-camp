STEM + Sports Programs

Every program turns movement into a learning lab.

Robotics Relay

Campers build small robots and program them to complete obstacle-course missions.

🏀

Basketball Analytics

Soccer Engineering

Speed Science

Campers measure reaction time, sprint starts, heart rate, and recovery.
