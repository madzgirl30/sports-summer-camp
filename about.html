About Our Camp

Camp SparkMotion was created for kids who love to ask questions, move their bodies, and make new friends.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help kids connect science, technology, engineering, and math to the sports they already enjoy. We believe learning feels more exciting when students can see how ideas work in real life.

Campers are encouraged to be brave thinkers, kind teammates, and creative builders. No camper has to be the best athlete or the best coder. They only need curiosity and a willingness to try.

Camp Details

  • Ages: 8–13
  • Location: Community Learning Center
  • Dates: June 8–July 17, 2026
  • Hours: 8:30 AM–3:30 PM
  • Lunch and water breaks included
