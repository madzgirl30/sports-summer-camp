Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for families before registration.

Does my child need sports or coding experience?

No. Camp SparkMotion is beginner friendly. Campers are grouped by age and comfort level.

What should campers bring?

Comfortable clothes, closed-toe shoes, sunscreen, a refillable water bottle, and a positive attitude.

Is lunch provided?

Yes. Campers receive lunch and snacks. Families may also send food from home.

How is safety handled?

Activities are supervised, equipment is age appropriate, and water breaks are built into the day.
